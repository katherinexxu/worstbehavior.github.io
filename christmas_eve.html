Christmas Eve

I wished for a snowy night on your birthday.

December night fall upon us fast, but you held my hand

and said, "let's go."

The way you smiled after picking me up.
The way you held my hand to warm it up even though yours was colder.
The way your eyes carried so much light and warm.
Step by step,
we walked to the LIRR Station. Interslocking our hands so tight.

Step by step,
we walked on clouds. Thinking we reached heaven

Step by step,
we walked towards your home. My safe haven.

Step by step...step by step...step by step...I looked at you and said
“I love you.”
